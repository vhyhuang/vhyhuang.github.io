about

I'm a passionate and driven mechanical engineering student that combines an interest in communications and project management with mechatronics and sustainable energy. I love to learn and sharpen my creative and analytical skills, since both of these sides of me help me become a better problem solver.

Since beginning my studies at the University of Toronto, I've worked on several engineering projects, become a student leader to mentor first year students, and had the honour of completing an internship at KPMG. I am skilled in coding in Python and MATLAB. I also have experience with breadboarding, soldering, machining, and designing with Solidworks.

In my spare time, I love to go out biking and I enjoy digital art and photography.
