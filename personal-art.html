my art

Since the age of 6, I've been involved in the arts. I took piano and theory lessons and earned my Royal Conservatory of Music Grade 10 certification with First Class Honours before entering university. In high school, I successfully auditioned for the Arts Westmount dramatic arts and visual arts programs, and graduated from the dramatic arts program.

Most recently, I completed a daily drawing challenge in May 2020, and took that opportunity to hone my skills in digital art. In addition to drawing, I use Adobe Photoshop for photomanipulation and post processing of my photography.

My art style focuses on use of colour, light, and composition for the creation of dynamic pieces. I hope to continue to pursue this interest alongside developing my engineering career.
